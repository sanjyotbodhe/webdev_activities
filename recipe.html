PANCAKE

INGREDIENTS

  • 1.5 cup all purpose flour
  • 3.5 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 tablespoon white sugar
  • 0.25 teaspoon salt., or more to taste
  • 1.5 cups milk
  • 3 tablespoons melted butter
  • 1 large egg

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. gather all ingredients
  2. mix baking powder, flour, sugar and salt
  3. make a well and add milk, butter and egg until its smooth
  4. heat a pan and pour one scoop of the mixture and flatten it out
  5. flip and cook until its browned on both sides
Serve and enjoy
